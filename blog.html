Empowering Young Minds: The Success Story of our Tech Innovators

Published on January 15, 2025

At Clemz Big Brain Academy, we believe in nurturing curiosity and innovation among our students. Recently, a group of our talented students participated in the National Tech Innovators Challenge held in Lagos. Leading the team was 15-year-old Aisha Bello, whose creative project on renewable energy earned the top prize.

The team developed an affordable solar panel system designed for rural communities, improving access to electricity. Aisha shared, "This experience has unlocked my passion for engineering and social impact. I want to continue creating solutions that make a difference."

This victory exemplifies our commitment to project-based learning, equipping students with practical skills and real-world experience that prepare them for the future.
